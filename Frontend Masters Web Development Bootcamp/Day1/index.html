How to make a PB&J sandwich?

  1. Get some peanut butter, jelly, and two slices of bread.
  2. Spread some peanut butter on top of one slice of bread.
  3. Spread some jelly on top of the other slice of bread.
  4. Put the two slices of bread together, joining the peanut butter and jelly.
